Who We Are

UFO is an event management agency headquartered in Mumbai, which has been operating since 2013.

Our mission is to provide our clients the most comfortable and amazing experience at their programs. Leaving no stone unturned to make the moments memorable for life. We build a relationship with our clients that goes long and never ends with one evening.

Experienced

In action since 2013, UFO has already made a prominent mark in the Television and Event Industry of India. We are a team of well trained and experienced individuals who never fail to deliver the promises

Well Networked

Over the years, we have organised events of a wide range around the country with all the who’s who of the industry. Be it from Bollywood, TV or of Sports Arena, we have a broad network which can cater all your needs.

Value for Money

In the world of events, we constantly thrive to add value to your vision without being harsh to your pocket. We provide the most bang for buck service in the market without any compromise in the qualty.

Passionate & Hard Working

When a team of dynamic, creative, dedicated and passionate professionals come together, be sure that you will have a solution for almost each and every problem. We never leave a stone unturned to make sure we provide the best service possible.
